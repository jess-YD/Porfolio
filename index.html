About Me

I am currently a student at Eleven Fifty Academy thriving to become a web developer. I aspired to study policy analysis and minor in management for my undergrad. It all went so well until I had to come back home for personal family reasons. In my mind I knew that if I did not attend college, I would never be happy in life because it was a personal fulfillment for me. Putting my education on hold, I worked my way up in the service industry. After 7 amazing years in the hotel/tourism and restaurant industry, I wanted to further my skills and challenge myself to overcome now bigger obstacles, since I managed to go beyond my daily duties and talents in my previous workforce. Coding was introduced to me by a friend who is currently attending Eleven Fifty and is very much passionate about becoming apart of the tech world. Doing something out of my comfort zone is definitely a challenge, but knowing that it has an immense support group and numerous of opportunities, I was intrigued to become a junior web developer. Of only being here for a couple of weeks, I have truly stretched my mind to learn and flourish one step at a time. Nights have been restless and dreams have been about coding, but it's exciting and effortful knowing that it means something, which is you never stop learning. Furthermore, becoming apart of the coding world would be invigorating in which I would be proud of being in. Hello, Coding World.
